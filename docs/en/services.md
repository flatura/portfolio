# Services & Approach

I work as a hands-on System Analyst and Technical Designer. My primary role is to bring structure to complex or chaotic environments, providing a clear roadmap for development and deployment.

## Rescuing and Scaling AI Prototypes
AI-assisted coding is a massive accelerator, but it often produces fragmented architecture. I help founders take their generated MVP and refactor it into a production-ready product. This includes setting up clear boundaries between the UI, APIs, databases, and external services, ensuring the code can be safely updated and deployed to a VPS or cloud environment.

## System & Architecture Design
I translate unclear business goals into a structured system blueprint. This involves defining the domain model, mapping out user scenarios, and creating architectural artifacts (C4, Sequence Diagrams) that serve as a direct guide for engineering. I build working prototypes if it is the fastest way to validate a solution.

## Data Modeling & Access Control
I design robust data models and integration contracts (APIs, ETL specifications). A key focus is on security and context-aware Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Instead of relying on generic defaults, I design granular access models tailored to your business logic (for example, isolating responsibilities into specific roles like `PLATFORM_ADMIN` and `PLATFORM_SUPPORT`).

## Where I Bring the Most Value
* You have an AI-generated MVP that needs architectural stabilization before launch.
* Requirements are raw, contradictory, or constantly changing.
* You need to design robust data structures, APIs, and integrations from scratch.
* The project requires strict traceability, auditability, and operational constraints.
