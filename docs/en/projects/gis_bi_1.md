# GIS Internal Systems Visualization

## Summary
B2E GIS platform for visualizing reports from internal enterprise tabular systems with spatial binding to railway infrastructure and the track-segment graph.

## Role and Contribution
Systems Analyst. Elicited and formalized functional requirements, prepared project documentation, and translated business needs into implementation tasks for developers.

Also implemented ad-hoc technical solutions, including geospatial data processing, tile server configuration, GIS router setup, and pre-production readiness activities.
